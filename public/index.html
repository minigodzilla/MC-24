Minto Communities' Story

Our story began in 1955 with a simple belief: that we could build better places to live, work and play, one home and one relationship at a time. Since then, The Minto Group has successfully expanded into western Canada, entering the Calgary market in July, 2012. Our dedication to the Calgary market keeps us focused on our unique approach to building communities that are sustainable, healthy and vibrant.
